A simple daily cycle

Not another habit list.

Intend focuses on the person behind the habit. The practice is short enough for an ordinary day and specific enough to change what you notice.

01

Choose your direction

Define the version of yourself you want to embody and one visible direction for today.

02

Practice for 20 seconds

Pause, breathe, visualize the shift, and let the intention feel present rather than distant.

03

Notice the proof

Record an observation, a useful action, or an old pattern. Every honest check-in becomes evidence.

Daily ritual
